Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What types of businesses need network security?

Any business with internet access, data storage, or internal networks should invest in network security—especially if handling sensitive information or complying with industry regulations.

 Do you provide on-site or remote support?

We offer both. Depending on your needs, our team can deploy on-site engineers or manage and monitor your network remotely with 24/7 support.

How do I know if my current network is secure?

We offer a Free Network Security Assessment to identify potential vulnerabilities and gaps in your existing infrastructure.

Can you help us comply with ISO 27001 or GDPR?

Yes. Our solutions are designed around global standards like ISO 27001, NIST, and GDPR to help you achieve and maintain compliance.

What tools and technologies do you use?

We use industry-leading tools such as FortiGate, Wazuh SIEM, pfSense, Ubiquiti, and open-source security frameworks based on client needs and budget.
